My work

Ethereal Portraits – A study of light, shadow, and human emotion

2024

This series of moody and cinematic portraits explores how light and composition can enhance storytelling. Each image is carefully crafted to bring out the subject’s personality and mood, using a mix of natural light and studio lighting techniques.

Tools Used:

Camera: Canon EOS R5

Lenses: 50mm f/1.2, 85mm f/1.4

Lighting: Godox AD600, Softbox & Reflectors

Editing: Adobe Lightroom & Photoshop

Wanderlust Landscapes – Capturing the raw beauty of nature

2023

A travel photography project featuring breathtaking landscapes from Norway, Iceland, and the Swiss Alps. This collection focuses on the interplay of light and natural elements to create dramatic, high-contrast compositions.

Tools Used:

Camera: Sony A7R IV

Lenses: 16-35mm f/2.8, 70-200mm f/4

Drone: DJI Mavic 3

Editing: Adobe Lightroom, Capture One

Editorial Fashion Shoot – "Urban Elegance"

2023

A high-fashion editorial shoot in downtown Berlin, blending industrial architecture with contemporary style. This project involved collaborating with stylists and models to create a cohesive visual aesthetic.

Tools Used:

Camera: Fujifilm GFX 100S

Lenses: 110mm f/2, 45mm f/2.8

Lighting: Profoto B10, LED Panels

Editing: Adobe Photoshop, Capture One

Documentary Series: "Life in Motion" – Candid moments from everyday life

2022

A black-and-white street photography series capturing authentic moments of people in urban settings. Shot across multiple cities, the focus was on movement, expressions, and fleeting interactions.

Tools Used:

Camera: Leica Q2 Monochrom

Lenses: 28mm f/1.7

Editing: Adobe Lightroom, Silver Efex Pro
