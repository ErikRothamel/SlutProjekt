About Me

Hello! I’m Erik Rothamel, a passionate photographer with a deep love for capturing moments that tell a story. Ever since I picked up my first camera, I’ve been fascinated by how photography can freeze emotions, preserve memories, and showcase the beauty of the world from unique perspectives. That passion has shaped my journey and continues to inspire my work every day.

My journey into photography started with a curiosity for light, composition, and storytelling. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in portrait photography, landscape photography, and documentary-style shooting, working on projects that challenge me to see beyond the lens and truly connect with my subjects. I thrive in environments where I can experiment with new techniques, collaborate with creatives, and push my artistic boundaries, and I’m always eager to learn and adapt to new challenges.

My Philosophy

At the core of my work is a belief in authenticity and emotion. Whether capturing a fleeting expression, a breathtaking landscape, or the raw energy of an event, I aim to create images that resonate, inspire, and evoke feeling. Photography, to me, is not just about taking pictures—it’s about storytelling, perspective, and the power of a single moment.

Whether it’s creating timeless portraits, exploring new environments through my lens, or capturing the essence of a brand through commercial photography, I love bringing ideas to life in ways that are both artistic and meaningful.

I’m excited about where this journey continues to take me and always looking forward to new opportunities to create, innovate, and grow.
