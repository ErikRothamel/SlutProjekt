Work

Welcome to my portfolio! Here, you’ll find a selection of my photography projects, each capturing a unique story, emotion, or moment in time. From portraits to landscapes, commercial shoots to personal creative endeavors, my work reflects my passion for visual storytelling.

Featured Projects

📸 Ethereal Portraits – A study of light, shadow, and human emotion

📍 2024 This series of moody and cinematic portraits explores how light and composition can enhance storytelling. Each image is carefully crafted to bring out the subject’s personality and mood, using a mix of natural light and studio lighting techniques. 🛠 Tools Used: Camera: Canon EOS R5 Lenses: 50mm f/1.2, 85mm f/1.4 Lighting: Godox AD600, Softbox & Reflectors Editing: Adobe Lightroom & Photoshop 🔗 [View Project]

📸 Wanderlust Landscapes – Capturing the raw beauty of nature 📍 2023 A travel photography project featuring breathtaking landscapes from Norway, Iceland, and the Swiss Alps. This collection focuses on the interplay of light and natural elements to create dramatic, high-contrast compositions. 🛠 Tools Used: Camera: Sony A7R IV Lenses: 16-35mm f/2.8, 70-200mm f/4 Drone: DJI Mavic 3 Editing: Adobe Lightroom, Capture One 🔗 [View Project]

📸 Editorial Fashion Shoot – "Urban Elegance" 📍 2023 A high-fashion editorial shoot in downtown Berlin, blending industrial architecture with contemporary style. This project involved collaborating with stylists and models to create a cohesive visual aesthetic. 🛠 Tools Used: Camera: Fujifilm GFX 100S Lenses: 110mm f/2, 45mm f/2.8 Lighting: Profoto B10, LED Panels Editing: Adobe Photoshop, Capture One 🔗 [View Project]

📸 Documentary Series: "Life in Motion" – Candid moments from everyday life 📍 2022 A black-and-white street photography series capturing authentic moments of people in urban settings. Shot across multiple cities, the focus was on movement, expressions, and fleeting interactions. 🛠 Tools Used: Camera: Leica Q2 Monochrom Lenses: 28mm f/1.7 Editing: Adobe Lightroom, Silver Efex Pro 🔗 [View Project]

This is just a glimpse into my work. Feel free to explore each project in detail, and if you’re interested in collaborating, don’t hesitate to reach out!
